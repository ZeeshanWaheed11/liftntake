Lift N Take Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2024

Lift N Take ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information from visitors to liftntake.com and from customers who schedule junk removal or property clean up services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details. Name, phone number, email address, and service address provided during booking or inquiry.
  • Service information. Notes about the items to be removed, photos you share, access details, and schedule preferences.
  • Payment data. Processed securely through our payment provider. We do not store full credit card numbers on our systems.
  • Website usage. Non-identifying data such as pages visited, browser type, and device information captured via standard analytics tools and server logs.

2. How We Use Information

  • To schedule pickups, confirm arrival windows, and deliver requested services.
  • To send appointment reminders, invoices, and follow-up updates.
  • To improve our website, customer experience, and marketing.
  • To comply with legal obligations and maintain accurate business records.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell or rent personal information.
  • Trusted partners such as payment processors, disposal facilities, or technology platforms may receive data only as needed to provide services on our behalf.
  • We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights or the safety of our team and customers.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use limited cookies and analytics scripts to understand site traffic and measure campaign performance. You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website features.

5. Data Retention & Security

  • Service records are retained as long as necessary to fulfill the job, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.
  • We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method is 100% secure, but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to safeguard your data.

6. Your Choices

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of marketing messages at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions or contacting us directly.

7. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. Review their policies before providing personal information.

8. Children

Lift N Take services and website are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us and we will remove it.

9. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at liftntake.com/privacy.html with a new effective date. Continued use of our services after an update constitutes acceptance.
